Node Mcu ESP8266 Wifi Repeater

1. Download Flash Tool from 
https://www.espressif.com/en/support/download/other-tools

2. Download wifi repeater firmware
https://github.com/martin-ger/esp_wifi_repeater

3. Upload firmware via flas tools

4. Reconnect Node mcu

5. Connect to MyAP wifi ssid. This is the node mcu new wifi AP. 

6. Go to http://192.168.4.1/ and set your wifi ssid credntials. This will connect home wifi and share internet 
over NodeMcu MyAP access poin

7. Connect with mobile or any device to MyAP wifi access point and you can share the internet.
